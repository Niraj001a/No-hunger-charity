Our Work For the Hunger

Feeding Orphan child

We as our team goes and visit the Orphanage home and feed the hunger child with the help of money we are provided by the people. We also go to visit the street places where the people are dieing out of hunger to help them as much as we can and we will help till our last breath.

Feeding Oldage home

Many old mothers and fathers are staying out home due to their sons and faimly thrown them out of home. They do not get 2 time per day daily food. We as our Team go to visit that kind of places and help them with needy things and food to eat as much as possible to provide them from our Charity.

Feeding Streetside

We visit the street side and village side where many children sleep with hungry stomach mostly. Due to hunger they also get ill and comes under some medical issues. So, to feed them it is one of important task for our Charity team as out motto is to No hunger should be left in our country.
